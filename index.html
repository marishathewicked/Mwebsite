About me

Back

Who am I? I'm a person who can't live without sunshine. I'm a person of sun. I'm energetic and my power will be sufficient for everybody. My favourite book is "The Little Prince" by Antoine de Saint-Exupery. My favourite color is the color of grass after rain. I can't imagine a day without music. I'm crazy about chocolate.

I like writing and reading. I like to feel the taste of words. I like coding. In the same way you can enjoy a good book, you can enjoy a good code. As one word or sentence expressing a brilliant idea can be cited over generations, you can create a universe building a sequence of code lines.
